Is there Earthquake Weather?

"All that is Earth has once been sky;" - C. S. Lewis, "The Meteorite"

Earthquakes are caused by the movement of tectonic plates in Earth's crust. However, there are also theories of quakes being triggered by heat, rainy weather, major storm systems, and even meteors landing on Earth's surface.
